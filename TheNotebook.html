The Notebook (2004)

⭐ 7.8/10 600K votes
2h 3min • Drama/Romance • Rated PG-13
"Behind every great love is a great story."
★ Rate This Movie

Story

A poor yet passionate young man falls in love with a rich young woman, giving her a sense of freedom, but they are soon separated because of their social differences. Set in the 1940s, the story follows their enduring love that spans decades.

In a modern-day nursing home, an elderly man reads this love story from his notebook to a fellow resident who suffers from dementia. As he reads, we see the flashbacks of young love, separation, and reunion, ultimately revealing that the story is their own.
