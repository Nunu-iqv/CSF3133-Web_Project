About PopCornPrise

Your trusted companion in the world of cinema since 2026. We're more than just reviews - we're a community of passionate movie lovers.

Our Story

PopCornPrise was developed as a group project based on a simple idea: to create a movie review platform where every opinion matters. Founded in 2026 by Group 5, the project aims to provide honest and unbiased reviews for all types of movies.

The group identified a gap in existing movie review platforms, where many reviews lacked authenticity and genuine audience perspectives. As a result, PopCornPrise was designed to be a community-driven platform that encourages users to share real experiences and opinions.

Through this project, PopCornPrise represents our commitment to authenticity, active community participation, and comprehensive movie coverage, making it a reliable source for movie reviews.

Our Core Values

Authenticity

We never accept paid reviews. Every rating and review comes from genuine viewers who have actually watched the movies.

Community First

Our platform is built by movie lovers, for movie lovers. We actively listen to our community and implement their suggestions.

Global Perspective

We celebrate cinema from every corner of the world, from Hollywood blockbusters to indie gems and international cinema.

Educational Focus

We believe in helping people understand and appreciate the art of filmmaking through our reviews and analysis.
